About this site

One of the big problems with other sites found on the internet is that they use a central server to compress your photos, which creates a big problem: the time it takes to upload and then download the photos back.

To solve that problem, I created this solution, where the photos are treated directly in the browser, without the use of any central server, thus guaranteeing the complete security of all your files and the maximum compression speed.
